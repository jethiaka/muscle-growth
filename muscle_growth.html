Training Nutrition Recovery

Training

To build muscle, focus on compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, bench presses, and pull-ups. Aim for 8-12 reps per set for hypertrophy and progressively increase the weight you lift.

  • Train 3-5 times a week with sufficient intensity.
  • Include both compound and isolation exercises.
  • Maintain proper form to prevent injuries.

Nutrition

Nutrition is key for muscle growth. Consume a diet rich in protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates.

  • Protein: Essential for muscle repair and growth. Aim for 1.6-2.2g per kg of body weight daily.
  • Carbohydrates: Provide energy for workouts. Choose whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Fats: Support hormone production. Include sources like nuts, seeds, and avocados.

Recovery

Muscles grow during rest, not while training. Ensure proper recovery to maximize gains.

  • Sleep: Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep every night.
  • Hydration: Drink enough water to support recovery and performance.
  • Active Rest: Include light activities like walking or yoga on rest days.
